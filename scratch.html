Quilting

One thing to know about me: I love to quilt! There are many things that go into my love of quilting.

  1. Calming
    • Lots of time to get in the flow and relax
    • Listen to music or audiobooks
  2. Creative
    • Learn about and play around with color theory
    • Make something beautiful and functional
  3. Impact
    • A personal gift for friends and family
    • Something to be passed down
